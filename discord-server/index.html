    Dsicord Server

    Freemcserver.net has an official discord server where you can get help from the community. To join click here.

    Rules

    The rules of the server have their own separate page, click here to view them.

    Channels

    We have a lot of different channels on the discord server. I will be listing all of the public channels under their own required role.

    Everyone:

    • support (Can get support on anything not related to account issues)
    • general (Just general talk about minecraft)
    • assistant-plugin (Support only on the FMCS Assistant Plugin not any other plugin)
    • offtopic (Talk here about anything not related to freemcserver or minecraft)
    • android-open-beta (Talk here about the Android Open Beta of the fremcserver app)
    • bot-commands (All bot commands should be executed here and not in any other channel)

    Connected:

    • connected (A channel for those who are connected to discord)

    Permissions

    We have a few permsissions allowed by those with the correct role.
